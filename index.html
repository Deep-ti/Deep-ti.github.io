About Me

My journey into software development began with a curiosity for how complex systems work behind the scenes. That curiosity quickly evolved into a passion for building reliable, scalable, and meaningful applications. Over the past 5+ years, I’ve had the opportunity to work as both a backend and full-stack engineer, contributing to enterprise-scale solutions in domains like telecom, financial services, and product management.
